01_LETTER
ELLIS,

Happy birthday. I hope this
next year gives you the same
kind of joy that you give so
naturally to everyone around
you.

You have one of those
presences that stays with
people. You make things feel
brighter, funnier, softer, and
more alive just by being there,
and that is a rare kind of gift.

I want May 16 to feel like a
real celebration of you: your
heart, your energy, your beauty,
your chaos, your softness, and
all the little things that make
you exactly Ellis. More laughter.
More unforgettable memories. More
days that love you back properly.
